COSMIC VINYL

3D AUDIO GALLERY

Welcome to Cosmic Vinyl!
A 3D audio space controlled by hand gestures.
We recommend using a camera to experience finger-guided flying interactions.
BACKGROUND MUSIC
Yes

Step 1: Open Palm & Slide

Spread your fingers and wave your hand left or right in front of the camera to scroll and browse through the 3D vinyl gallery.

Step 2: Hover to Focus

Keep your hand still on a specific record. It will zoom in closer and show you the song name and details.

Step 3: Make a Fist to Play

Once focused, clench your hand into a fist and hold still for 2 seconds. Wait for the circular ring to load, and the record will fly into the turntable!

Step 4: Open Palm to Return

While music is playing, spread your fingers again to fly the record back to the wall and resume browsing other tracks.
